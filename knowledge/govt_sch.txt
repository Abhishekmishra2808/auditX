Important Ratios 
Liquidity Ratios (Short-term repayment ability) 
 Current Ratio = Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities
     Ideal for MSMEs: 1.33 : 1 or higher
Solvency Ratios (Long-term stability)
Debt–Equity Ratio = Total Outside Liabilities ÷ Net Worth
    Acceptable range:
Manufacturing: ≤ 2 : 1
Trading / Services: ≤ 3 : 1 
Profitability Ratios (Earning strength)
Net Profit Ratio = Net Profit ÷ Sales × 100
 Expected:
Manufacturing: 5%–10%+
 Trading: 2%–5%
Operating Profit Ratio = Operating Profit ÷ Sales × 100
Debt Servicing Ability 
DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) = Cash Accruals ÷ (Term Loan Installment + Interest)
                       Minimum required: 1.25
                       Comfortable: 1.5+
Efficiency Ratios (Operational discipline)
Inventory Turnover Ratio = Cost of Goods Sold ÷ Average Inventory
Debtors Turnover Ratio = Credit Sales ÷ Average Debtors
Promoter Strength Ratio
TOL / TNW Ratio = Total Outside Liabilities ÷ Tangible Net Worth
Preferred: ≤ 4

*Important points
Liquidity ratio
Current Ratio = Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities
Solvency Ratios
a)Debt Equity Ratio = Total Outside Liabilities ÷ Net Worth
i) Total outside liabilities = Long term borrowings+ long term provisions
ii) Net worth = share capital + Reserve and surplus 
Profitability Ratios
Net Profit Ratio = Net Profit ÷ Sales × 100
i) Net profit = Gross profit - Indirect expenses 
ii)Sales = cash sales + credit sales
Operating Profit Ratio = Operating Profit ÷ Sales × 100
i)Operating profit = Gross + other operating income - other operating expenses 
ii)Sales = cash sales + credit sales
Debt Servicing Ability 
DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) = Cash Accruals ÷ (Term Loan Installment + Interest)
Efficiency Ratios
a)Inventory Turnover Ratio = Cost of Goods Sold (cogs)÷ Average Inventory
i) cogs= opening inventory+ net purchases+ direct expenses - closing inventory 
ii) Average inventory= opening inventory+ closing inventory ÷ 2
Debtors turnover ratio = Credit Sales ÷ Average Debtors
i) credit sales= net sales - cash sales 
ii) Average Debtors= opening debtors + closing Debtors ÷2
Promoter Strength Ratio
TOL / TNW Ratio = Total Outside Liabilities ÷ Tangible Net Worth
 i) Total Outside Liabilities  = Long-Term Liabilities + Current Liabilities
ii) Tangible Net worth = Net Worth − Intangible Assets
Break-Even Analysis
Break-Even Point (Units) = Fixed Cost ÷ (Selling Price − Variable Cost)
Break-Even Point (Sales Value)= 
Break-Even Units × Selling Price
Maximum permissible bank finance (MPBF)
i) MPBF = Current Assets − Current Liabilities − 25% of Current Assets
